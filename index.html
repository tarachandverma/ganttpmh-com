Gantt Project Management House is young organization, inculcating skill sets of Project Management in Engineering professionals and students. Our crew members are from IITs and NITs, have delivered industry based projects efficiently

 

 

Project Planning and Control

From scratch, we generate Project Plan, resource requirement and earned value analysis. We maintain controlling and monitoring of project throughout project duration.

 

Training

Professional courses (Primavera, MS Project, Staad Pro, Pro-E, Catia, Revit, Catia, PPM, PMP, Autocad) are infused into students, individual professional and corporate.

 

Project Management Consulting

We have a team of engineers, render end to end consulting services ranging from feasibility study, BOQ preparation, estimation, Structural Designing, MEP designing, Architectural Planning, Liasoning issue, site execution, Budget planning & cash-flow management, maps and drawing for individual house, multi-storey building.
