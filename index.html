Gantt Project Management House is young organization, inculcating skill sets of Project Management in Engineering professionals and students. We have team of faculties from IITs and NITs, have delivered industry based projects efficiently through certain skill sets:-

 

 

Project Planning and Control

From the scratch of ground preparation of Project Plan, resource requirement and earned value analysis. We maintain controlling and monitoring of project throughout project duration.

 

Training

Professional courses (Primavera, MS Project, Staad Pro, Pro-E, Catia, Revit, Catia, PPM, PMP, Autocad) are delivered to students, individual professional and corporate.

 

Project Management Consulting

We have a team of engineers, renders consulting services ranging from feasibility study from ground, BOQ preparation, estimation, Structural Designing, MEP designing, site execution, Budget planning & cash-flow management, maps and drawing for individual house, multi-storey building.
